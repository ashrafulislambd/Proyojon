✅ Core Features Every BNPL System Should Have
1. User / Customer Management

User profile (name, contact, email, address)

KYC identity verification (optional / secure)

Phone and email verification

Login / signup with strong authentication

Ability to upload documents

2. Merchant / Vendor Management

Merchant onboarding

Merchant verification

Merchant product catalog

Merchant settlement history

3. Product & Catalog

View eligible products for BNPL

Product filters (price ranges, brands, etc.)

Product details page

4. BNPL Purchase Flow

Add to cart

Choose BNPL as payment method

Show repayment schedule

Interest, late fee, total due calculation

Real-time eligibility checks

5. Payment Plan Options

Offer multiple repayment plans:
✔ 3-month
✔ 6-month
✔ 12-month
✔ Custom plan (optional)

Include:

Monthly due amount

First payment date

Interest breakdown

6. Payment / Transaction History

Record of each purchase

List of paid vs unpaid invoices

Payment receipts

Export statements

7. Real-Time Balance & Due Alerts

Dashboard showing outstanding balance

Push and email reminders before due dates

Penalty/late fee notices

8. Notifications

✔ SMS / Email alerts
✔ App notifications for:

Payment reminders

Payment successful

Plan changes

Failed payment alerts

9. Admin Panel

For your RDBMS backend:

User management

Merchant management

Transaction approvals

Audit logs

Reports & analytics

10. Security

Password hashing + salting

SQL injection protection

Token authentication

Secure storage of sensitive data
